EQUIPMENTS:
Tank | A tank for storing liquid
Basin | A Basin is a physical structure or containment unit designed to hold and condition a volume of water as part of a treatment process. It has at least one inlet and one outlet for water flow and may include drains, overflow paths, mixing devices, or aeration elements depending on its operational role.
Reactor | An equipment used for chemical reaction | SubEquip: Tank
SequencingBatchReactor | A type of activated sludge process for wastewater treatment | SubEquip: Reactor | Process: Process-ActivatedSludge
PlugFlowReactor | A type of reactor where the fluid flows in one direction through the tube | SubEquip: Reactor
ContinuouslyStirredTankReactor | A reactor in which contents are well mixed and reactants are added continuously | SubEquip: Reactor
StaticMixer | A device for mixing liquids without moving components | SubEquip: Reactor | Process: Process-Mixing
AerationBasin | A tank where water is aerated to remove gases and volatile organic compounds | SubEquip: Reactor, Basin | Process: Process-Aeration
CoagulationBasin | A basin where coagulation occurs to destabilize particles in water | SubEquip: Basin | Process: Process-Coagulation
FlocculationBasin | A tank where flocculation occurs to aggregate particles in water | SubEquip: Basin | Process: Process-Flocculation
Digester | A container to promote decomposition of organic waste | SubEquip: Tank | Process: Process-Digestion
AnaerobicDigester | A container to promote decomposition of organic waste in anaerobic conditions | SubEquip: Digester | Process: Process-AnaerobicDigestion
AerobicDigester | A container to promote decomposition of organic waste in aerobic conditions | SubEquip: Digester | Process: Process-AerobicDigestion
DisinfectionUnit | A unit used to eliminate or reduce harmful microorganisms | SubEquip: Tank | Process: Process-Disinfection
ChlorinationUnit | A unit that uses chlorine or chlorine compounds for disinfection. | SubEquip: DisinfectionUnit | Process: Process-Chlorination
UltravioletLightUnit | A unit using ultraviolet light for disinfection | SubEquip: DisinfectionUnit | Process: Process-UVDisinfection
ChlorinationBasin | A basin where chlorine is added for disinfection | SubEquip: ChlorinationUnit, Basin
SedimentationTank | A tank used to remove solids from liquids through sedimentation | SubEquip: Tank | Process: Process-Sedimentation
ImhoffTank | A sedimentation tank specifically designed for septic treatment | SubEquip: SedimentationTank | Process: Process-Sedimentation
Screen | An equipment used for separation | Process: Process-Screening
GritChamber | A chamber used to remove grit from wastewater | Process: Process-Sedimentation
Reservoir | A large natural or artificial body of water used for water supply
Pond | A body of still water smaller than a lake, used for treatment or storage
Wetland | A marsh or bog-like area that is permanently or seasonally saturated with water, used for pre- or post-treatment.
Battery | A device that stores energy for later use
Cogenerator | An equipment that produces both electricity and heat from the same energy source | Process: Process-Cogeneration
Boiler | A device for heating water | Process: Process-Incineration
Conditioner | An equipment used to prepare raw biogas from a digester by removing impurities
Flare | A device used to burn off unwanted gas
Pump | A device used to move fluids by mechanical action
PressureExchanger | A device used to transfer pressure energy from one fluid to another
DewateringUnit | A unit used to remove water from solid material | Process: Process-Dewatering
Thickener | A device used to increase the solids concentration of a slurry
DissolvedAirFlotationThickener | A thickener that uses dissolved air to separate solids from liquids | SubEquip: Thickener | Process: Process-Flotation
CentrifugalThickener | A thickener that uses centrifugal force to separate solids from liquids | SubEquip: Thickener | Process: Process-Centrifugation
GravityThickener | A thickener that uses gravity to separate solids from liquids | SubEquip: Thickener | Process: Process-Sedimentation
BeltThickener | A thickener that uses a belt system to separate solids from liquids | SubEquip: Thickener | Process: Process-Filtration
GravityBeltThickener | A thickener that combines gravity separation with a belt system | SubEquip: BeltThickener, GravityThickener
RotaryDrumThickener | A thickener that uses a rotating drum to separate solids from liquids | SubEquip: Thickener | Process: Process-Filtration
Filter | An equipment used to remove impurities from liquids or gases | Process: Process-Filtration
ReverseOsmosisMembrane | A membrane used for reverse osmosis | SubEquip: Filter | Process: Process-ReverseOsmosis
RotatingBiologicalContactor | Fixed-film process using a slowly rotating discs partially submerged in a tank | SubEquip: Filter | Process: Process-Biofiltration
TricklingFilter | A filter system that treats wastewater by trickling it over a bed of rocks or plastic | SubEquip: Filter | Process: Process-TricklingFiltration
OxidationDitch | Modified activated sludge process that uses a ring-shaped channel to biologically remove pollutants | SubEquip: Reactor | Process: Process-ActivatedSludge
MembraneBioreactor | A filter system that combines a membrane process like microfiltration with a biological reactor | SubEquip: Filter | Process: Process-MembraneBioreactor
MicrofiltrationUnit | A filter system which removes contaminants from a liquid by passing it through a microporous membrane | SubEquip: Filter
UltrafiltrationUnit | A filter system that uses a pressure-driven barrier to separate particles and solutes in a fluid | SubEquip: Filter
NanofiltrationUnit | A filter system that uses a membrane to soften water and remove organic contaminants | SubEquip: Filter
MediaFiltration | A filter system that uses a bed of material to filter out contaminants | SubEquip: Filter
RapidSandFilter | A type of media filter that uses sand as the filter medium and operates at high filtration rates. | SubEquip: MediaFiltration | Process: Process-RapidSandFiltration
CartridgeFiltrationUnit | A filter system using a cartridge to remove contaminants from fluids | SubEquip: Filter
IonExchangeMembrane | A membrane that selectively allows ions to pass through while blocking other substances | Process: Process-IonExchange
ElectrodialysisUnit | A unit that uses electricity to drive ion movement through a membrane | Process: Process-Electrodialysis
Electrolyzer | An equipment that uses electricity to split liquids into constituent elements | Process: Process-Electrolysis
Crystallizer | An equipment used to produce solid crystals from a solution | Process: Process-Crystallization
Compressor | An equipment that increases the pressure of a gas
Evaporator | A device used to turn the liquid form of a substance into its gaseous form | Process: Process-Evaporation
Condenser | A device used to condense a gaseous substance back into a liquid | Process: Process-Condensation
VariableFrequencyDrive
MultifunctionalMembrane | A membrane integrating multiple processes (e.g., reduction, adsorption, filtration) for selective removal and recovery of contaminants. | SubEquip: Filter
MolybdenumSulfideBasedMembrane | A specific material demonstrating effectiveness in removing heavy metals and oxyanions with high selectivity. | SubEquip: Filter
ElectricallyConductingMembrane | A membrane capable of conducting electricity, used for in-situ cleaning or electro-oxidation processes. | SubEquip: Filter
ElectroDialyticCrystallizer | A modular system designed for brine concentration and crystallization, integrating electrodialysis and crystallization. | Process: Process-Electrodialysis, Process-Crystallization
Electrode | A component where electrochemical reactions occur in electrified processes.

BiologicalAeratedFilter | A filter that uses biological processes to remove contaminants. | SubEquip: Filter | Process: Process-BiologicallyActiveFiltration
ElectrocoagulationUnit | A unit that uses electrocoagulation for contaminant removal. | Process: Process-Electrocoagulation
OzonationUnit | A unit that uses ozone for water treatment. | Process: Process-Ozonation
GranularActivatedCarbonAdsorber | A unit that uses granulated activated carbon to adsorb impurities from water. | Process: Process-GranularActivatedCarbon

PROCESSES:
ProcessType | A process applied to water treatment.
Physical-Process | A unit process that relies on physical forces to separate or treat water.
Process-Separation | The most generic process for separation of any two constituents. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Elutriation | A process that uses a stream of fluid flowing upward to separate a mixture of particles based on their size, shape, and density. | SubProcess: Process-Separation
Process-Screening | A unit process that removes large solids from water, such as leaves, branches, and other debris. | SubProcess: Process-Separation
Process-Sedimentation | A unit process that allows solids to settle out of water by gravity. | SubProcess: Process-Separation
Process-Filtration | A unit process that removes smaller particles from water by passing it through a filter. | SubProcess: Process-Separation
Process-MediaFiltration | A filtration process that uses a bed of media (as opposed to a membrane-based process). | SubProcess: Process-Filtration
Process-SlowSandFiltration | A filtration process that uses a bed of sand to remove impurities at low flow rates. | SubProcess: Process-MediaFiltration
Process-RapidSandFiltration | A filtration process that uses a bed of sand to remove impurities at high flow rates. | SubProcess: Process-MediaFiltration
Process-Flotation | A unit process that uses buoyancy to separate solids from water, often using air bubbles. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-GasTransfer | A unit process that transfers gases, such as oxygen or carbon dioxide, into or out of water. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Aeration | A unit process that aerates (i.e., transfers oxygen into water). | SubProcess: Process-GasTransfer
Process-Mixing | A unit process that mixes water with chemicals or other substances to achieve a desired effect. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Equalization | Flow and load dampening using a tank or basin | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Dewatering | A process to remove or separate water from another material, typically solids. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Centrifugation | A process that uses centrifugal force to separate substances of different densities. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Solidification | A process of turning a liquid into a solid (e.g., freezing). | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Crystallization | A process of forming solid crystals from a solution. | SubProcess: Process-Solidification
Process-Evaporation | A process of turning a liquid into a vapor. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Condensation | A process of turning a vapor into a liquid. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-Adsorption | A process by which substances bind to a surface. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-GranularActivatedCarbon | A process that uses an activated carbon filter to adsorb impurities from water. | SubProcess: Process-Adsorption, Process-Filtration
Process-MembraneProcess | A process that uses a semi-permeable membrane to separate substances. | SubProcess: Physical-Process
Process-ReverseOsmosis | A core membrane separation process for desalination and advanced purification. | SubProcess: Process-MembraneProcess
Process-ClosedCircuitReverseOsmosis | A specific RO configuration explored for optimization, capable of adapting to unmeasured changes like feed salinity. | SubProcess: Process-ReverseOsmosis
Process-OsmoticallyAssistedReverseOsmosis | A process with validated Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) models for cost optimization. | SubProcess: Process-ReverseOsmosis
Process-FeedReversalReverseOsmosis | A dynamic and cyclic design to mitigate scaling and balance salt/foulant load, enabling high recovery. | SubProcess: Process-ReverseOsmosis
Process-MembraneDistillation | A thermal membrane process, often subject to CFD modeling for optimization of heat and mass transfer. | SubProcess: Process-MembraneProcess
Process-Electrodialysis | A process that uses ion-exchange membranes and an electric potential to separate ions from water. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process, Physical-Process
Process-ElectroDialyticCrystallization | A novel integrated process combining electrodialysis and crystallization for energy-efficient Zero-Liquid Discharge (ZLD) by maintaining a saturated brine stream. | SubProcess: Process-Electrodialysis, Process-Crystallization
Process-Comminution | Process of reducing solid materials into smaller particles through mechanical forces such as crushing, grinding, impact, shear, or compression. | SubProcess: Process-Physical
Process-BiosolidsDisposal | Generic process for unspecified disposal of biosolids (e.g., incineration, land application, landfilling, etc.). | SubProcess: Process-Physical
Chemical-Process | A unit process that involves the addition of chemicals to water to achieve a desired effect.
Process-ChemicalAddition | Addition of chemicals, such as coagulant, polymer, disinfectant, etc. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process
Process-Coagulation | A unit process that adds chemicals to water to cause small particles to clump together. | SubProcess: Process-ChemicalAddition
Process-Electrocoagulation | An electrified pretreatment technology using sacrificial anodes to release coagulant precursors for contaminant removal. | SubProcess: Process-Coagulation, Physical-Process
Process-Flocculation | A unit process that gently mixes water to allow coagulated particles to form larger clumps. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process, Process-Mixing
Process-UVDisinfection | A disinfection process that uses ultraviolet light to inactivate microorganisms. | SubProcess: Process-Disinfection
Process-Disinfection | A unit process that uses chemicals or other methods to kill or inactivate harmful microorganisms in water. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process
Process-Chlorination | A disinfection process that uses chlorine or chlorine compounds. | SubProcess: Process-Disinfection
Process-Dechlorination | A post-disinfection process that removes excess chlorine compounds. | SubProcess: Process-Disinfection
Process-pHAdjustment | A unit process that adjusts the pH of water to a desired level using chemicals. | SubProcess: Process-ChemicalAddition
Process-Neutralization | pH adjustment to neutral, typically with a base, used in processes like high-density sludge treatment. | SubProcess: Process-pHAdjustment
Process-Oxidation | A unit process that adds oxidizing agents to water to remove contaminants or improve water quality. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process
Process-AdvancedOxidation | Processes combining UV light with oxidants (e.g., UV-AOP) to destroy contaminants. | SubProcess: Process-Oxidation
Process-Electrooxidation | An advanced oxidation process often combined with EC to enhance contaminant removal and inactivation, particularly for viruses. | SubProcess: Process-Oxidation, Physical-Process
Process-Ozonation | A water treatment process that uses ozone as an oxidant. | SubProcess: Process-Oxidation
Process-Softening | Chemical precipitation of calcium/magnesium | SubProcess: Process-ChemicalAddition
Process-ChemicalPrecipitation | Removal of nutrients or metals using coagulants | SubProcess: Chemical-Process
Process-Reduction | Mechanism by which contaminants are transformed through the gain of electrons. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process
Process-SolventExtraction | A non-membrane, non-thermal method using a solvent to selectively extract water from highly saline brines. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process
Process-HighDensitySludge | A method for treating acid mine drainage involving neutralization. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process, Physical-Process
Process-IonExchange | A process in which ions are exchanged between a solution and an ion-exchange resin or membrane. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process, Physical-Process
Process-Electrolysis | A process that uses a direct electric current to drive an otherwise non-spontaneous chemical reaction. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process
Process-Incineration | A process that combusts biosolids or biogas. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process, Physical-Process
Process-FluidizedBedIncineration | A process that combusts a constantly moving bed of biosolids. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process, Physical-Process
Process-MultipleHearthIncineration | A process that combusts biosolids as they move through a series of stacked hearths with controlled air flow. | SubProcess: Chemical-Process, Physical-Process
Process-Cogeneration | A process that combusts biosolids or biogas to produce electricity and heat simultaneously. | SubProcess: Process-Incineration
Biological-Process | A unit process that utilizes biological activity for water treatment.
Process-Nitrification | Process by which ammonia is oxidized to nitrite and subsequently nitrate. | SubProcess: Biological-Process
Process-Denitrification | Process by which nitrate is deoxidized to nitrogen gas and nitrogen oxides. | SubProcess: Biological-Process
Process-Bardenpho | A five-stage biological nutrient removal process specifically designed for nitrogen and phosphorus. | SubProcess: Biological-Process
Process-ModifiedLudzackEttinger | A two-stage biological nutrient removal process designed for nitrogen removal through anoxic denitrification followed by aerobic nitrification.
Process-ActivatedSludge | Aerobic suspended-growth biological treatment | SubProcess: Biological-Process
Process-Biofiltration | A filtration method that uses biological processes to remove contaminants (e.g., trickling filter). | SubProcess: Biological-Process, Process-Filtration
Process-TricklingFiltration | Trickling filter using any media (e.g., plastic, sand, gravel, etc.) | SubProcess: Process-Biofiltration
Process-BiologicallyActiveFiltration | A biologically activated filtration column. | SubProcess: Process-Biofiltration
Process-MembraneBioreactor | Combined biological and membrane filtration | SubProcess: Process-Biofiltration
Process-Digestion | A biological process that breaks down organic matter over a long duration in a tank. | SubProcess: Biological-Process
Process-AnaerobicDigestion | A biological process that breaks down organic matter in the absence of oxygen. | SubProcess: Digestion
Process-AerobicDigestion | A biological process that breaks down organic matter in the presence of oxygen. | SubProcess: Digestion
